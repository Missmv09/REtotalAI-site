What’s the difference between Growth and Professional?

Growth is perfect if you’re a solo investor or agent who wants to underwrite every lead and use the core toolset on a regular basis. Professional is for operators and teams who want everything unlocked — including deeper Renovation Estimator options, all new tools, and faster support when deals are moving quickly.

Can I start on Starter and upgrade later?

Yes. Most users start on Starter, run a few deals, and then upgrade to Growth once they see how REtotal AI fits into their workflow. You won’t lose your account or history when you upgrade.
